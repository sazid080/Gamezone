Game of the Year 2022

Elden Ring

"Elden Ring" is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware.Elden Ring is anticipated to offer the intricate and challenging combat mechanics haracteristic of FromSoftware's titles,along with a deeper emphasis on exploration and a branching narrative that provides players with choices affecting the game's outcome.

Game of the Year 2018

GOD OF WAR

God of War is an action-adventure video game series originally developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The series primarily follows the story of Kratos, a former Spartan warrior seeking vengeance against the gods of Olympus.

Game of the Year 2019

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

"Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice" is an action-adventure game developed by FromSoftware, known for its challenging and immersive gameplay. Set in a dark and fantastical interpretation of late 1500s Sengoku Japan, the game follows Wolf, a shinobi with a mission to rescue his kidnapped lord and seek revenge against his enemies.

Game of the Year 2017

THE LEGEND OF ZELDA:BEATH OF THE WILD

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild" is an action-adventure game that redefines the iconic Zelda series. Developed by Nintendo, it immerses players in a vast, open-world realm of Hyrule, offering a breathtaking landscape where exploration, discovery, and adventure reign supreme.

Game of the Year 2020

The Last of Us Part II

"The Last of Us Part II" is a highly anticipated action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog, serving as a sequel to the critically acclaimed "The Last of Us." The narrative continues in a post-apocalyptic world, exploring the emotional and complex journey of Ellie, a young survivor, seeking justice and grappling with the repercussions of her actions.
